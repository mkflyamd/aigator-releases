# AI Gator

### Your Integrated Work Environment

AI Gator brings your entire work life into a single, intelligent interface. Chat with your data, automate your workflows, and take action across every tool you use — all without leaving one place.

---

## What is an IWE?

An **Integrated Work Environment** connects your apps, data, and AI in one unified experience. Instead of switching between a dozen tools, AI Gator lets you ask, act, and automate — across email, calendar, documents, chat, code, and the web — in a single conversation.

---

## What AI Gator Can Do

### Communication & Collaboration
- **Email** — Read, search, compose, reply, and forward. Every action goes through a review pane before anything is sent.
- **Microsoft Teams** — Read channels and chats, search messages, and send messages with your approval.
- **Slack** — Search conversations, read threads, and draft messages for your review before posting.
- **Contacts** — Find, create, and manage contacts across your organization.

### Calendar & Scheduling
- Check your schedule, find free slots, and detect conflicts
- Create meetings, invite attendees, and set recurring events
- Set out-of-office blocks and manage cancellations
- Fully timezone-aware — no mental math required

### Documents & Office Files
- **Word** — Create documents, edit content, update tables, and toggle form checkboxes
- **Excel** — Build workbooks, write formulas, run calculations, and format data
- **PowerPoint** — Generate presentation decks from scratch with professional layouts and design
- **OneDrive & SharePoint** — Browse, search, upload, and share files
- **OneNote** — Create notebooks, add pages, and read content

### Web & Research
- Search the web and extract information from pages
- Navigate to URLs and interact with forms
- Run complex multi-step browser tasks autonomously
- Every browser action is paused for your review before execution

### Project & Issue Tracking
- **Jira** — Search issues, create tickets, add comments, transition statuses, and log time
- **Confluence** — Read wiki pages, create and edit content, navigate spaces
- **GitHub** — Review pull requests, check CI status, manage issues, and leave comments

### Scheduling & Automation
- Schedule any AI task on a recurring basis using natural language
- Set interval or cron-based jobs that run in the background
- Track execution history, token usage, and job status

---

## Built for Safety

Every action that matters requires your approval:

- Emails are **drafted, never auto-sent**
- Teams and Slack messages go to a **compose pane first**
- Calendar events are **confirmed before creation**
- File deletions and contact changes require **explicit confirmation**
- Browser interactions **pause for your review**

AI Gator is built on the principle that AI should assist, not act unilaterally.

## Skill Marketplace

Extend AI Gator with skills from the built-in marketplace. Skills add new capabilities — from specialized document workflows to custom integrations — and are organized into trust tiers: Native, Verified, Community, and Enterprise.

## Download & Install

1. Download  from the [latest release](https://github.com/mkflyamd/aigator-releases/releases/latest)
2. Run the installer — no administrator rights required
3. AI Gator appears in your system tray and launches automatically
4. Open Settings to connect your accounts and configure your LLM provider

No Python installation required. AI Gator ships with a fully embedded runtime.

---

## Updates

AI Gator checks for updates automatically on startup and prompts you when a new version is ready. Updates are delivered over-the-air with zero manual steps.
